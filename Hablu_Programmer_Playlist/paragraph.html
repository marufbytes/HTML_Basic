A paragraph is a distinct unit of writing, comprising several sentences, that develops a single, central idea or topic.

It's typically marked by an indentation or a line break and helps to organize and structure text, making it easier for readers to follow an author's argument.
Effective paragraphs are unified by their focus on one idea, coherent in their logical arrangement of sentences, and well-developed by providing sufficient explanation and support for their controlling idea.
